Privacy Policy

Welcome to Anywhere Map. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses and protects information while providing map navigation and mock location features.

Important Notice

Anywhere Map uses Android's official Mock Location framework. Users must manually enable Developer Options and manually select Anywhere Map as the Mock Location application. The app cannot enable these settings automatically.

Information We Collect

Anywhere Map does not require users to create an account or sign in. We do not collect personal information such as your name, phone number or email address through the application. All app settings remain on your own device.

Permissions Used

The application may request the following Android permissions to provide its core functionality.

  • Precise Location Permission
  • Background Location Permission
  • Notification Permission
  • Internet Permission
  • Network Access
Permissions are completely controlled by the user. You can grant or revoke permissions at any time through Android Settings.

Location Services

Anywhere Map uses your device location to display maps and simulate locations selected by you. Background Location permission allows location simulation to continue while the application is running in the background.

Developer Options

The application requires users to manually enable Android Developer Options and manually select Anywhere Map as the Mock Location application. The app cannot enable these settings automatically.

Battery Optimization

For reliable background operation, some Android devices may require Battery Optimization to be disabled for Anywhere Map. This setting is optional and remains under the user's control.

Notifications

Anywhere Map may display a foreground service notification while location simulation is active. These notifications are used only for proper operation of background services.

Advertising

Anywhere Map is a free application supported by Google AdMob advertisements. Google may collect certain non-personal information such as advertising identifiers and device information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

The application does not operate its own servers for storing user information. App settings and preferences remain locally on your own device.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Maps SDK
  • Google AdMob

Children's Privacy

Anywhere Map is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future updates will be published on this page.
