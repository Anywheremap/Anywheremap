Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using Anywhere Map. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application uses permissions, how information is processed, and how we protect your privacy while providing location simulation, map navigation and related services.

Important: This application provides location simulation features using Android's official Mock Location framework. Users must manually enable Developer Options and manually select Anywhere Map as the Mock Location application. The app cannot enable these settings automatically.

Information We Collect

Anywhere Map is designed to work without requiring users to create an account or sign in. We do not require usernames, passwords, phone numbers, or any personal profile information.

The application does not operate its own servers to collect or store your personal information. Your selected locations, preferences, and settings remain on your own device unless otherwise required by Android system services.

Permissions Used

Anywhere Map requires certain Android permissions in order to provide its core functionality. These permissions are used only for features requested by the user.

  • Precise Location Permission
  • Background Location Permission
  • Notification Permission
  • Internet Access
  • Network State Access
Granting permissions is completely controlled by the user. Permissions can be revoked at any time through Android Settings.

Location Permission

Anywhere Map requires access to your device location in order to display maps, show your current position, allow destination selection, and provide accurate location simulation features.

Precise Location

The application requests precise location permission so the map can determine your real device location before location simulation begins.

Background Location

If you choose to continue using Anywhere Map while the application is running in the background, Background Location permission may be required. This permission allows the app to continue updating the simulated location while the screen is off or while another application is being used. Background location is used only after you explicitly start location simulation.

Background Location is never enabled automatically. Users must manually grant this permission from Android Settings. You may revoke this permission at any time.

Developer Options & Mock Location

Anywhere Map uses Android's official Mock Location framework. To use the core functionality of this application, users must manually:

  • Enable Android Developer Options.
  • Open the Developer Options screen.
  • Select Anywhere Map as the Mock Location Application.

The application cannot enable Developer Options, cannot select itself automatically, and cannot modify Android system settings without your permission. All configuration is performed manually by the user.

Battery Optimization

Some Android devices restrict background activity in order to save battery power. If you wish to continue using simulated location while the application is running in the background, you may choose to disable Battery Optimization for Anywhere Map. This step is optional and remains completely under the user's control.

Notification Permission

Anywhere Map may display a foreground service notification while location simulation is active. This notification is required by modern Android versions to keep background location services running correctly. The application does not use notifications for advertising or promotional messages.

Advertising

Anywhere Map is a free application supported by advertising. Advertisements are provided through Google AdMob. Google and its advertising partners may collect certain non-personal information, such as advertising identifiers, device information, and approximate location, to display relevant advertisements and measure advertising performance.

For more information about how Google uses information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Maps SDK

Anywhere Map uses Google Maps SDK to display maps, allow location selection, search places, and improve navigation functionality. Use of Google Maps services may be subject to Google's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Data Collection

Anywhere Map does not maintain its own servers for collecting, storing, or selling users' personal information. Your selected locations, favorites, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your own device. The developer does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Anywhere Map may use the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Maps SDK
  • Google AdMob

Each third-party provider operates under its own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We encourage users to review those policies for additional information.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting user privacy. Anywhere Map does not intentionally collect or store personal information on developer-owned servers. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to help protect information processed by the application. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and therefore absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Rights

As a user of Anywhere Map, you have full control over your device permissions. At any time you may:

  • Revoke Location Permission.
  • Disable Background Location Permission.
  • Disable Notification Permission.
  • Stop Mock Location.
  • Clear the application's local data through Android Settings.
  • Uninstall the application at any time.

Your choices will not affect your ability to remove the application from your device.

Children's Privacy

Anywhere Map is not designed for children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the application, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.
